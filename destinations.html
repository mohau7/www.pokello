Maldives island

Stranded in the vastness of the Indian Ocean, the small island nation of the Maldives is like paradise on earth. Two-hundred-twenty of the country's 1,200 islands are populated, with 87 being open for tourism. There are no crammed beaches or fights over deckchairs here, just guaranteed sea views. Life here has adjusted to the year-round warm weather. With an average temperature of 28 celcius, the Maldives offer a break from the stresses and strains of everyday life year round.
